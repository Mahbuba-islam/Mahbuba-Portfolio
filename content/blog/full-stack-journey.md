I finished my HSC in Bangladesh in 2015, and a decade later I'm in New York,
finishing Programming Hero's full-stack bootcamp and writing this blog.

## What clicked

The thing that finally made web development click for me was building a
project end-to-end — designing the schema, writing the API, and shipping the
UI all in one weekend. Every layer informs the others, and that loop is what I
love.

## What I'm focused on

- **Real-time systems** — chat, notifications, live collaboration.
- **AI features** — small, useful integrations baked into ordinary products.
- **Polish** — accessibility, motion, and typography that respects the user.

If you're hiring a junior full-stack intern in NYC (or remote-friendly), I'd
love to chat.
